Chinese Mianshi Gallery

An online archive that introduces the characteristics and history of Mianshi (pasta foods) from 7 regions of China.

This website is mainly show the characteristics and historical origins of typical mianshi from seven geographical regions of China. The criteria for the division of the seven geographical regions are based on the textbooks Physical Geography of China. Chinese people have a long history of eating mianshi. From the beginning of eating flour to the improvement of cooking utensils, people use more and more kinds of mianshi. There are also many kinds of mianshi popular in China, such as noodles, steamed bread, steamed bread and so on. Each region has evolved many special mianshi due to its geographical conditions and ethnic customs.

This site was built using Jekyll. All the code is available on GitHub.
